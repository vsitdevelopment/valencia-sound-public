Community Life

A Place to Belong

At Valencia Sound, you don't just purchase a home — you join a community with a shared enthusiasm for living life to its fullest. Our residents are warm, friendly, and welcoming, creating an inviting environment where lifelong friendships are made.

With dozens of clubs, events, and social activities, there's something for everyone — from art, book club, and theatre to music, cards, and more.

Facilities

The Heart of Valencia Sound

At the center of it all is our stunning 39,000 sq ft clubhouse — the heartbeat of the community. Whether meeting friends for dinner at the full-service restaurant, relaxing at the outdoor bar, or soaking up the sun by one of our three pools, you'll find endless ways to enjoy each day.

Active Lifestyle

Stay Energized & Fit

For those who love staying active, Valencia Sound features 11 lighted pickleball courts, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a variety of group exercise classes to help you stay energized and inspired every day.

From competitive league play to morning stretch classes — there is always a way to move, connect, and thrive here.

World-Class Facilities

Amenities & Recreation

Over 30 world-class amenities designed to keep you active, involved, and inspired — every day of the year.

Social Spaces

Amenities Designed
for Your Lifestyle

Meet, relax and enjoy our lifestyle at the 39,000 square-foot Clubhouse which hosts live entertainment, shows, events and seasonal parties. Three game rooms and a social hall offer space for everything from poker and canasta to mahjong and more. A fully equipped demonstration kitchen and an arts and crafts room are additional places to learn, play and create.

Community Involvement

A Club for
Every Interest

A variety of resident-run clubs are available to match every interest — from pickleball, tennis, softball, and bocce to book clubs, current affairs, cultural activities, arts and crafts, performing arts, poker, canasta, mahjong and hearts. Currently there are more than 30 sanctioned clubs.

Sports

Where Great Play
Meets Great Company

The Racquet Center features 11 lighted Pickleball courts, 6 lighted Har-Tru Tennis courts and 4 lighted Bocce courts. Three experienced sports pros are available for private lessons and group instruction.

Health & Wellness

Experience Community
in Every Space

Stay active at the state-of-the-art Fitness Center, located in our Clubhouse. It offers top-tier cardio equipment — including treadmills, spin and recumbent bikes, and elliptical machines — as well as a full range of free weights and strength-training machines. Certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions. The separate group exercise studio is home to a variety of classes including Zumba, Pilates and Stretch and Balance.

Aquatics

Splash Into the Season
With Your Neighbors

The four resort-style pools feature a zero-entry main pool, a lap pool, a resistance pool, and even a kiddie pool for visiting grandkids. And of course, an outdoor hot tub, as well as an outdoor pool bar and poolside dining.

Dining

Where Great Food
Meets Great Company

Our full-service restaurant, located in the clubhouse, offers fresh, chef-curated lunch and dinner menus and specials. Our sports bar, adjacent to the restaurant, offers Happy Hour pricing, bar bites and a full restaurant menu. Live entertainment is featured periodically to enhance your night out.
